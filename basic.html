SUBWAY SURFERS.

Subway Surfers is a singleplayer game played on your mobile devices. You control your character as it runs away from a security guard through a railroad track. The goal of this game is to kill time by playing a silly game and getting new high scores. There are constantly new events and many skins/skateboards to collect. It was created in May 23, 2012 by SYBO founders Sylvester Rishøj Jensen and Bodie Jahn-Mulliner.
